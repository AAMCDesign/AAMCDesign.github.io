Artwork

SELLOUT

During my first semester of my sophomore year at UMass Dartmouth I had focused a semester long study on the artist Shepard Fairey. At the end of the semester we were allowed to either write a 5 page essay or create a piece inspired by the artist we were studying on. This is my piece dedicated to Fairey which true to him was appropriating his most successful piece "OBEY" with my own likeness.

F Bodoni

This is a piece that was created during my Typography class at UMass Dartmouth dedicated to the font "Bodoni". Through several itterations of failed compositions I had created this out of pure frustration and it was the most interesting concept I had created. With a few touchups it became a statement poster rather than something that is simply visually appealing.

BEETLE

A faux company logo created for a graphic design project at UMass Dartmouth. The focus of the project was to create an identity for a product of our choosing with the stipulations that it must be vector artwork and based off of some form of insect. With rhinosaurus beetle being my insect of choice with the product of carmine (a natural red dye) as the focus I decided to include it in the artwork itself.

8ight

A Typography project focused on the serifs and identity of fonts as a whole and being able to show such things with as little information as possible. With countless version of this style being made, this version held itself against the rest by allowing the viewer to have enough information to see the connections amongst them while also seeing the beauty within their own spaces.
